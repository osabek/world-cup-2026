🔑 Score source

Leave blank to use the free built-in score feed (no key needed — works everywhere, including the hosted version). Or paste a free football-data.org API key to use the official-data API instead (key mode only works when running the local server on this Mac).

🔊 Sounds

Goal horns, whistles and crowd roars.

💾 Scenario slots

Save the current scenario under a name and switch between different "what-if" worlds. Real synced results are shared by all slots.

🧹 Wipe all scores

Clears everything — your scenario picks and the real results — for a totally fresh start. Real results will be restored automatically the next time you press Sync or when Live updates are on.

ℹ️ How to play

Tap the + and − buttons to set scores in the groups — the tables update instantly and the top teams jump into the bracket. In the bracket, just tap a team to send them through! Real results that come in from Sync/Live get a 🔒 lock — tap the ✏️ to play "what-if" with them anyway. Click any flag to meet the players! ⚽
